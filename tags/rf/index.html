RF clock setting the time manully

Oct 29, 2017 #rf  #clock 
Press and hold the M.SET button for 3 seconds to set the time: Press the M.SET button for 1 second to advance 1 minute. Press the M.SET button for more than 1 second to auto advance the minute hand. If the M.SET button is not pressed for 8 seconds, or press REC button, the clock is set. Start manual reception Press the REC button for 3 seconds. The hands will move to the 12:00 position and the clock will start searching for the DCF signal. …
