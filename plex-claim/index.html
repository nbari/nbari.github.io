plex claim

December 19, 2025

To claim your Plex server, (for example in proxmox), modify /var/lib/plexmediaserver/Library/Application\ Support/Plex\ Media\ Server/Preferences.xml

Add the following line inside the <Preferences> tag:

allowedNetworks="10.13.0.0/23"

This will allow Plex to be claimed from the specified network range.
