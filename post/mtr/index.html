mtr on specific port:

mtr -w -c 100 -b -T -P 587 smtp.server.tld > smtp-mtr.txt

The -w -c 100 -b -T runs a verbose report 100x, logging both IPs & Hostnames, for a good dataset. This also runs the queries through TCP, to avoid ICMP de-prioritization.
